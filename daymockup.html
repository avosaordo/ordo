Note on UAE National Day
  • Mass :the Collect "For the Nation or State" (Various Needs n. 21) may replace the Opening Collect of the Mass

  • Prayer of the Faithful: a suitable intention may be included for the country and rulers

  • Outside of Mass : the Order for a Blessing in Thanksgiving (Book of Blessing 1969-1979) may be followed, substituting the intercessions with the Te Deum (Holy God, we praise thy name or another vernacular paraphrase) or the Magnificat. The Concluding Prayer may be taken from one of the Votive Collects of the Missal (Various Needs nn. 21-24).

  • N.B. Other patriotic manifestations (e.g. national anthem) should take place outside of Mass and preferably outside of the church building. Ceremonies of a religious character, asking for the divine blessing/guidance of the nation is permitted within the church.

Notes on the Solemnity
  • External Solemnity :one or more Masses of St. Francis Xavier may also be celebrated on the following Friday/Saturday with the permission of the Bishop

  • Indulgence: A plenary indulgence under the usual conditions is granted to those who visit the parish church of St. Francis Xavier, and there pray an Our Father and the Creed (Manual of Indulgences 33.5a).
